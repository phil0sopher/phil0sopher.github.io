Log-In/Sign-Up

I decided to fix-up Coursera's sign-up and sign-in module. The log-in and sign-up buttons originally didn't indicate they were tab buttons. I also didn't like how the fill-boxes didn't have any margins, to indicate a slight difference in what was meant to be filled out. Also I fixed up the rounded edges to fit with the rest of their brand.

Credit Card Form

I took a trip to Europe for the first time in March 2017 and Norwegian Airlines happened to be my airlines. To match their great service and design guidlines, I wanted to embody the feeling of the airline trip experience using inspiration from plane tickets. I also wanted to keep their minimalistic design and tried to keep it, while having the bare essentials in the form.

Music Player

I wanted to tackle a new design platform and get my creative juices going after going on a short hiatus. So I mixed my love of reading and combined that with interaction/UX design to create an audiobook player. Keeping the user in mind, I designed the screen to be easily accessible for audiobook listeners on the move; the navigation is simplified with easily accessible buttons and the text is aligned with the focus box to be easily read on the go. Also the aestheics try to embody being in a library and reading in solitude.
